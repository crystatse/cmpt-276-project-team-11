Purpose

The goal of our project is to develop a web application that helps users with writing their research papers. This application uses AI to let users to look for recently published papers, search for papers written by specific researchers, find papers similar to the ones they have used, and generate citations and bibliographies for their sources. In addition to accessing papers, AI will also be used to summarize and answer questions about the papers. This application aims to streamline the process of finding information for research by cutting down the time spent looking for relevant papers.

What Can You Do With ReasearchBuddy?

See Recently Viewed and Recently Published Papers
On ResearchBuddy's homepage, users can see their recently viewed papers along with a list of recently published papers below the search bar.

Search for Relevant Papers
Users can use the search bar to find papers related to their research. Search prompts could include paper topics, authors, and DOIs.

Summarize Quickly and Ask Questions
Using the OpenAI API, users can quickly summarize the contents of a paper with the click of a button. With a split-screen view of a selected paper and an AI chatbot, users can read and ask ChatGPT questions about the paper on the same screen.

Find Similar Papers
Along with summarizing, users can also click a button to find papers similar to their selected paper.

Cite Papers
Users can quickly cite the selected paper in a specified format by clicking the "Cite" button above the chatbot.
